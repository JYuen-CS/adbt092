Travel and Collect

Text, Shape and Waterfall

Travel and collect is a platform game and the goal of the game involves collecting the right number of gems in order to enter the portal successfully. There are spirits (or blue fireballs you can call it) that act like thieves that want to steal the gems you collected. Collect the gems or else get trapped inside the level.

You can check out the code on Github: https://github.com/JYuen-CS/adbt092
